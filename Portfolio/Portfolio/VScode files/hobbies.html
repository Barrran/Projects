Hobbies

There are many wonderful things these days that can take up so much of our free time. Sometimes finding what you like can seem like an impossible task. I have been fortunate enough, however, to have been able to explore numerous different aspects of our world and have found a few things I cannot do without. Here is a list of my favorite hobbies, in no particular order.

  • AI Generated Art
  • Auto racing
  • Video Games
  • Horticulture
  • Culinary Arts

AI Generated Art

AI Generated art has won me over! I love the freedom and creativity that AI opens. There are so many different rabbit holes to follow when it comes to AI assisted art.

Auto Racing

I've always had a love for cars. Getting behind the wheel or watching them take corners, the thrill of auto racing makes my blood pump. Beauty and technology in one fluid concept.

Video Games

Since the early days of NES (the original Nintendo Entertainment System) I've been hooked. There is something about video games that transforms life into an extrodinary adventure.

Horticulture

Everything about plants makes me wonder. How do they grow? How do they adapt? How can I learn more? This has been a life long passion that has helped shape my life throught learning about nutrition.

Culinary Arts

This goes hand-in-hand with Horticulture. I grow the food, then I cook the food, then I eat the food. I do try to cook the food in a really appetizing way.
